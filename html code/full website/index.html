Welcome

First Fiddle Restaurants, formerly known as The Lazeez Affaire Group, was conceived in the year 1999 by Priyank Sukhija and Y.P. Ashok. The company is a renowned innovator and leader in the hospitality industry. Opened in the year 1999, the first outlet by the company- Lazeez Affaire, popularised the concept of fine dining in India. Following its success, places like Boulevard Café followed storming Delhi’s nightlife with Karaoke night. Eventually, The Flying Saucer Cafe captivated the metropolis with the famous Sufi nights. Other well-known brands include ‘Out of the Box’, ‘Boombox’, ‘Raas’, ‘Fork You’, ‘Scooter’, ‘RPM’, ‘1, Cafe Boulevard’, and others.
